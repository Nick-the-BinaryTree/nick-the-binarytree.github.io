In Model United Nations meetings, resolutions are written, debated, and voted upon.

Major conferences tend to ask that delegates submit a position paper about their country's stance on the topic they've been assigned.

About

Students take on the positions of diverse nations to debate current global issues and work out resolutions, emulating actual UN procedure. We also occasionally do historical events, such as Treaty of Versailles 1919.

We annually attend large Model UN conferences in New York City, diplomatically competing (It's difficult to describe) with other clubs. We're deciding from a wide range of conferences this year, containing events at venues including Ivy Leagues, the Grand Hilton, and private Manhattan high schools.
