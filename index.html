Introduction

Hi, I'm Nick. I'm studying computer science and math at the University of Connecticut. My programming stretches back to writing text adventures in 5th grade, and I'm currently a Facebook intern. Recently, I was the "Popular Choice" winner of the UConn Hackathon 2017.

In the past, I've been at CORE, Catapult Marketing, and MediaCrossing. Between them, I've had the privilege of working for a government institution, Fortune 500, and lean startup.

Organizations

I founded and served as Chief Strategy Officer of UConn's Model UN team. In my role, I taught public speaking and diplomacy, hosted UConn's first inter-collegiate MUN events, and acted as CMO. For my marketing initiative, I managed half-a-dozen recruitment channels including print, in-person, web, and social media. We were nationally ranked for the first time in school history under my administration.

In the second half of college, I shifted priorities to the university IT service, Cyber Security Club, and Finance Society.

Volunteering

I was a 5th Grade Sunday School teacher for 3 years and a librarian for 4 years. Most actively, I write open source software on GitHub and coding tutorials on Medium.
